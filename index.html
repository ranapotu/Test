The benefits of exercise during Pregnency

During pregnancy, your changing body and harmone levels present their fair share of challenges. But growing research shows that exercise helps you to adapt to these throughout pregnancy and even in labour.

It's a common myth that exercising during pregnancy is bad for your baby. In reality, the oposite is true. Here we take a closer look at the health benefits of exercise to you and your baby and what simple changes can make to have a fitter pregnancy.
